What Is the Tech Quiz?

It's a Telegram Mini App — a lightweight web game that runs inside your Telegram chat. You pick a category, answer 10 randomized questions with a 15-second timer, and try to rack up as many points as possible before running out of lives.

It's designed for quick sessions. A full game takes about 3 minutes, and you can play as many times as you want.

6 Categories

  • AI & Machine Learning — GPT, transformers, RLHF, tokens, temperature, and more
  • Web Development — HTML, CSS, React, TypeScript, Docker, GraphQL, CORS
  • Cryptocurrency — Bitcoin, DeFi, smart contracts, Layer 2, staking, whales
  • Tech History — iPhone, ARPANET, Python, Spotify, and computing pioneers
  • Cybersecurity — VPNs, phishing, zero-days, XSS, honeypots, encryption
  • Mixed — random questions from all categories

How Scoring Works

Each correct answer earns 10 base points plus a speed bonus (answer faster, earn more). Get 3+ correct in a row and you trigger a streak multiplier that can double your bonus per question. Wrong answers cost you a life — you start with 3 lives.

The best players consistently hit 80-100+ points by combining accuracy with speed and maintaining long streaks.

Global Leaderboard

Every score you submit goes to the global leaderboard. Type /top in the bot to see the current rankings. Your best score counts — play as many games as you want, and only your highest score shows on the board.

Challenge Friends

After each game, you can send a challenge directly to any Telegram contact. They'll see your score and can try to beat it. It's a simple way to start friendly competitions without any setup.

How to Play

  1. Open @charlie_dl_bot in Telegram
  2. Tap the Menu button (bottom left) to open the quiz
  3. Pick a category and hit Play
  4. Answer 10 questions before time runs out
  5. Save your score to the leaderboard
  6. Challenge friends or play again

Works on any device with Telegram — iOS, Android, or desktop.
