ClipDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026
Short version: ClipDeck collects and transmits no user data. Everything you save stays in your own browser. There is no server, no account, and no analytics.

What ClipDeck stores

  • The text you explicitly highlight and save, plus the page title and URL it came from.
  • This is saved locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local.

What ClipDeck does NOT do

  • It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.
  • It has no server, no account, and no analytics.
  • It only reads the text you actively select — never the rest of the page, your browsing history, or any other site content.

Permissions

  • storage — to save your highlights locally.
  • contextMenus — to add the right-click "save highlight" action.
  • downloads — to let you export your highlights as a Markdown file you choose to save.

Your control

Your clips live only in your browser. Use the "Clear all" button in the popup to delete them at any time. Uninstalling ClipDeck removes all stored data.
