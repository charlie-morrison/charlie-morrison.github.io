If you are choosing between Big Interview and Pramp, the short version is this: they overlap on the surface, but they are built for different job-search profiles. Big Interview is positioned as mock interview platform with AI feedback. Pramp is positioned as free peer-to-peer mock interviews (now Exponent).

Quick verdict

  • - Best free tier: Pramp (Free).
  • - Lowest cost: Pramp (Free).
  • - Big Interview is best for: People with a specific interview loop coming up in 2-4 weeks.
  • - Pramp is best for: Software engineers prepping technical loops on a budget.

What each one does well

Big Interview — what it does well

  • AI-scored answers with concrete improvement notes
  • Industry-specific question banks
  • Course-style curriculum included

Pramp — what it does well

  • Free real-human practice partners
  • Strong technical interview coverage
  • Built-in code editor for SWE rounds

Where each one falls short

Big Interview — where it falls short

  • Higher price tier than DIY alternatives
  • Course content can feel dated in places
  • Webcam-on requirement is a friction point

Pramp — where it falls short

  • Partner quality varies — you might get a beginner
  • Scheduling friction across timezones
  • Less coverage of behavioral than technical

Big Interview or Pramp: which one is right for you?

Pick Big Interview if you map to: People with a specific interview loop coming up in 2-4 weeks. The tradeoff you accept is the weak-spot list above — usually a category-specific gap rather than a deal-breaker.

Pick Pramp if you map to: Software engineers prepping technical loops on a budget. Same caveat — read the weak spots before you commit to a paid year.

If neither fits cleanly, this is the case where stacking the free tier of one with a different paid tool tends to win.

FAQ

Is Big Interview better than Pramp for ATS optimization?

It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". Big Interview (interview prep) and Pramp (peer interview prep) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Can I use both Big Interview and Pramp together?

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
