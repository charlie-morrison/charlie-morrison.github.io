FAQ

Is ChatGPT (for resume writing) better than Resume.io for ATS optimization?

It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". ChatGPT (for resume writing) (general AI) and Resume.io (resume builder) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Can I use both ChatGPT (for resume writing) and Resume.io together?

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
