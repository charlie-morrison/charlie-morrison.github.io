FAQ

Is Jobscan better than Resume Worded for ATS optimization?

It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". Jobscan (ATS scanner) and Resume Worded (ATS scanner + LinkedIn review) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Can I use both Jobscan and Resume Worded together?

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
