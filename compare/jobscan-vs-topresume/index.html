If you are choosing between Jobscan and TopResume, the short version is this: they overlap on the surface, but they are built for different job-search profiles. Jobscan is positioned as match-score your resume against a job description. TopResume is positioned as human resume writer + ATS scan service (per-resume pricing).

FAQ

Is Jobscan better than TopResume for ATS optimization?

It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". Jobscan (ATS scanner) and TopResume (human review service) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Can I use both Jobscan and TopResume together?

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
