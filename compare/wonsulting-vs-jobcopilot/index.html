It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". Wonsulting AI Jobs (AI job tools suite) and JobCopilot (auto-apply) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
