If you are choosing between Zety and Novoresume, the short version is this: they overlap on the surface, but they are built for different job-search profiles. Zety is positioned as resume + cover letter builder with AI suggestions. Novoresume is positioned as clean, ATS-tested resume templates with content guides.

Quick verdict

  • - Best free tier: neither — both gate core features behind paid plans.

  • - Zety is best for: First-time resume builders who want guided prompts.
  • - Novoresume is best for: ATS-cautious applicants who want a clean, parseable resume.

Zety or Novoresume: which one is right for you?

If neither fits cleanly, this is the case where stacking the free tier of one with a different paid tool tends to win.

FAQ

Is Zety better than Novoresume for ATS optimization?

It depends on what you mean by "ATS optimization". Zety (resume builder) and Novoresume (resume builder) approach the problem differently. If you want a literal match score against a job description, lean toward dedicated ATS scanners. If you want a clean, parseable layout, prioritize the tools that test ATS parsing.

Can I use both Zety and Novoresume together?

Yes, and many job seekers do. Use one for the job pipeline (tracking, autofill, or volume) and the other for the resume itself. The free tiers usually cover that split without paying twice.
