Stop staring at a blank calendar. Get a balanced 4-week posting plan — ideas, hooks, and CTAs — for your niche and platform in one click.

How this calendar is built

Random posting is why most accounts stall. This tool doesn't just spit out ideas — it enforces a balanced content mix so you're not accidentally posting five sales pitches in a row (the fastest way to kill reach). Every plan is weighted roughly:

  • ~35% Educate — teach one useful thing. Builds trust and gets saves/shares.
  • ~20% Engage — questions, polls, prompts. Feeds the algorithm the comments it rewards.
  • ~15% Behind-the-scenes — process, story, the messy middle. Humanizes the brand.
  • ~15% Inspire / Social proof — transformation and real results. Lowers buying resistance.
  • ~15% Promote — the actual offer. Kept to a minority so it lands when it appears.

Every post gives you three things

  • A content idea tailored to your niche.
  • A scroll-stopping hook for the first line (the only line that decides if anyone reads the rest).
  • A call-to-action and format/hashtag guidance tuned to your chosen platform.

Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored. Copy any post, or download the whole plan as CSV and drop it into your scheduler.
