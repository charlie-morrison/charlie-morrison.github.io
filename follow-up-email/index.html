Follow-Up Email Tips

  • Send your thank-you email within 24 hours of the interview
  • Reference something specific you discussed — it shows you were engaged
  • Keep it under 200 words. Interviewers are busy
  • If following up on a decision, wait at least 5-7 business days
  • Proofread twice. Typos in a follow-up email are worse than no email
  • Don't copy-paste a template verbatim — adjust the details to sound like you
