LinkedIn Headline Best Practices

  • Stay under 220 characters — LinkedIn truncates longer headlines on mobile
  • Front-load keywords — recruiters search by job titles and skills
  • Show value, not just title — "Helping SaaS companies reduce churn by 30%" beats "Customer Success Manager"
  • Use separators — pipes (|), bullets, or em dashes improve readability
  • Include measurable results — numbers catch attention in search results
  • Update every 3-6 months — keep it current with your latest achievements
