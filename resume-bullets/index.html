Resume Bullet Point Generator

Turn boring job duties into achievement-oriented bullet points that get interviews.

Pro tip: Hiring managers spend 7 seconds per resume. Bullet points starting with "Responsible for" get skipped. Bullet points starting with action verbs and including numbers get read.

Before → After Examples

❌ Responsible for managing social media accounts
✅ Grew Instagram following from 2K to 15K in 6 months through data-driven content strategy, increasing engagement rate by 340%
❌ Helped with customer service
✅ Resolved 50+ customer tickets daily with a 98% satisfaction rating, reducing average response time from 4 hours to 45 minutes
❌ Worked on the company website
✅ Rebuilt company website using React and Next.js, improving page load speed by 60% and increasing organic traffic by 25%
❌ Responsible for team meetings
✅ Facilitated weekly cross-functional standups for a 12-person team, reducing project blockers by 40% and improving sprint velocity by 20%
